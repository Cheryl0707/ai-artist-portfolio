=== PROJECTS — AI + UNREAL PRE-VIS ===

Client-facing pre-visualization for Samuel Adams campaigns (Octoberfest & Juicy IPA). === PROJECTS — AUTOMATION WORKFLOWS ===

Email Agent (2025)
A Slack-based email automation agent ("Mail BOT") that automates full email outreach workflow. Two core pipelines:
1. Fetch + Build Context: Connects to Gmail, pulls email history for contacts or groups (e.g. all @pepsi.co), uses AI to analyze relationships — generating structured summaries (background, collaboration history, last contact, studio visit evidence). Stored in Google Sheet as permanent relationship database.
2. Draft with AI & Template: Template-based quick drafts or AI-assisted contextual drafting — pulls relationship summary, generates three personalized openings, builds email paragraph by paragraph, each step reviewable before sending to Gmail draft box.
Designed to scale into a full Client Intelligence System with HubSpot CRM integration.
Tools: n8n, OpenAI API, Slack API, Gmail API, Google Sheets
